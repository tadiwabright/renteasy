About Tenant Network

Connecting landlords and tenants across Zimbabwe

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Tenant Network has become Zimbabwe's premier rental property platform.

We started with a simple mission: to make the rental process easier and more transparent for both landlords and tenants. What began as a small local service has grown into a nationwide network connecting thousands of property owners with quality tenants.

Our team is passionate about real estate and technology, and we're constantly innovating to improve your rental experience.

Our Mission

We're committed to:

  • Providing a secure platform for property listings
  • Streamlining the rental process
  • Offering transparent communication tools
  • Supporting both landlords and tenants
